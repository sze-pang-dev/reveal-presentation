AWS Services

1. Analytics

Why Analytics?

  • Getting information out of raw data and make decisions or answer questions

  • Data analytics can reveal trends and metrics that would otherwise be lost in the mass of information

  • This information can then be used to optimize processes to increase the overall efficiency of a business or system

Data Challenges

  • It is difficult to find, securely access and analyze relevant data sets

  • No one good place to find diverse data across industries and each provider delivers data differently

  • Providers must build and maintain technology to package and deliver data

2. Development

A Process

  • Process of creating, designing, deploying and supporting software

Development Challenges

  • Coding best practise is a common phrase but the actual implementation of the idea can be lacking

  • Poorly written code can be expensive to maintain or cause critical bugs

  • Bugs might go unoticed or are caught too late

3. Business Apps

A way of work

  • Business moves fast and having the right tool is important for teams to be efficient

  • Businesses use mass-produced packaged applications to manage workflow and teams

Not Made to Measure

  • Critical data lives in spreadsheets, emails and project management systems

  • Sharing data across multiple users and multiple spreadsheets is difficult

  • A management system comes as a one-size-fits all and may not be entirely suitable for all the teams needs

4. Translation

Why translate?

  • Translation services are needed to translate digital content to be able to access a variety of audiences

  • 55% of consumers buy only in their language

  • Reach is limited by the amount of languages an application is supporting

Translation Challenges

  • Localization is manually intensive

  • Businesses may have to hire a localization firm for a professional translator per language they are wanting to support

  • Manual translation can be slow and costly

5. Testing

Importance of Testing

  • Testing helps to identify errors, gaps, or missing requirements

  • Software bugs could be expensive or even dangerous

  • It ensures security, product quality, and customer satisfaction

Testing complexity

  • Increase of Device models, variations in firmware, OS versions and carriers

  • Various dependencies on remote services and other applications

  • Having to provision devices and manage a testing infrastructure
